Edie and Dale Johnson

Sales and Marketing and Professional Gambler.

Read more...

Dale and Edie Johnson came to Walla Walla in the early '70s, where both worked in the medical field. After retiring in their mid-30s, they traveled extensively while their children were young.
In recent years, they owned and operated Brickstone Design Gallery which, through its wine bar and shop, led them to a joint partnership with friends to plant a vineyard and make their own wine. Today, Edie and Dale still reside in the Walla Walla Valley, where they enjoy relaxing and biking around the local scenery. They have two beautiful, grown daughters, Brandy and Stacey.

As a custom home builder, Gary has an excellent reputation of style and perfection in whatever he sets his mind on doing.
